<ul>
    <li> This mod adds caracals to your world!</li>
    <li> They like chickens and rabbits</li>
    <li> You can tame them with raw fish or chicken/rabbit</li>
    <li> Spawns naturally in the savanna</li>
    <li> They have cute sounds!</li>
